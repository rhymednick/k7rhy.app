---

Pick the sound first, then build the guitar around it. Each voicing uses the same body and scale length but a different combination of pickups, switching, and controls.

A **Ready** badge means I've built and validated that voicing and its documentation is complete: sound reference, parts list, and wiring guide. **Lab** voicings have a finished design that I haven't physically validated yet, and **Concept** voicings are still exploratory — you're welcome to follow along on both, but don't order parts for them yet.
