The body is the foundation — print the parts, bond them, let the epoxy cure, then sand and seal the surface. Same body for every voicing.

## Steps

1. [Print](/relay/body/print) — Download the print files, choose your material, and get all parts off the bed before moving on.
2. [Bonding](/relay/body/bonding) — Bond the printed parts into a single complete structure with G/Flex 650 epoxy.
3. [Finishing](/relay/body/finishing) — Sand the bonded body and seal the surface before hardware installation.
