The body stage is the first physical thing you build — printing the shared double-cut body, bonding the front and back halves, curing, and finishing it. Same body for every voicing.

## What this guide will cover

- Print settings (material, layer height, walls, infill, supports, orientation)
- Print order and common failure modes
- Bonding the body halves (adhesive choice, surface prep, joining sequence)
- Clamping pattern and 24-hour cure
- Finishing — squish-out cleanup, sanding, optional paint or seal

## Status: in progress

We're actively writing this content as the first physical builds happen. Until the guide ships, the conversation is in Discord — drop in to follow along, share progress on your own print, or ask questions about the body design.
