## Pickup height and balance

The lipstick pickup is the most sensitive part of the balance. Start with the **humbuckers** on the **3-way** (neck / both / bridge) and set a level you like, then adjust the lipstick so it **adds** clearly when blended in without washing out the buckers.

## Common issues

**Buzz on specific frets:** Usually a high fret or insufficient neck relief. Check relief first.

**Buzz only on open strings:** Usually a nut slot issue — the slot may be too shallow (action too high) or too low (buzz on the open note).

**Volume imbalance between humbucker selector positions:** Adjust neck and bridge heights. **Lipstick-heavy or thin blend:** adjust lipstick height and re-check with the blend engaged. If humbucker balance is still wrong, verify coil split behavior on the push-push pots.

**Tone control has no effect:** Check the capacitor solder joint. Cold joints on cap legs are common.
