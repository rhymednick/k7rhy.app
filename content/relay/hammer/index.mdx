## Voice and vibe

Relay Hammer is the **least subtle** guitar in the family and does not pretend otherwise. **High-gain authority**, **tight aggressive attack**, and **rail-style passive pickups** — the **specialty brute** of the line, **not** the centerpiece. The **5-way** layout matches how many metal players use the instrument: **direct access** to distinct pickup combinations matters more than a minimalist switch.

Hammer should **not** be burdened with elegance. It should be **direct, forceful, and immediately legible** to the player it is built for.

## Who falls in love with this

**Metal and hard rock** players who want **tight low end**, **saturated sustain under heavy picking**, and a Relay that **owns** the high-gain lane. If you spend most of your time with gain staged and palm-muted and want a deliberate contrast to the warmer, more vintage-leaning siblings, Hammer is aimed at you.
