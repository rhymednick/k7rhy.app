## Character

Hammer is the one model in the lineup that isn't trying to be versatile. Rail humbuckers throughout, a Super Switch for position-specific coil routing as the design matures — the goal is tight, saturated, controlled aggression. If you want the Relay body and the Relay platform but your home is high-gain territory, this is the model built specifically for you.

## Who it's for

Metal and hard rock players. Hammer is the specialist of the lineup — it doesn't split the difference with cleaner voices, it just commits to the high-gain lane and does it well.
