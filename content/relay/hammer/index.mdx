## Voice and vibe

Hammer is the **outlier on purpose**. Where other Relay models chase versatility across clean and dirty contexts, Hammer is aimed at players who spend most of their time with gain staged and palm-muted. The design target is **tight low end, aggressive saturation, and sustain under heavy picking** — a specialty tool, not the compromise “do everything” guitar.

Picture dropped tunings, fast rhythm work, and leads that need to stay defined when the amp is pushing hard.

## Who falls in love with this

**Metal and hard rock** players who know what they want from high gain and are tired of guitars that only sound right at bedroom volume. Hammer is for builders who want a Relay that **owns** that lane — a deliberate contrast to the warmer, more vintage-leaning siblings in the family.
