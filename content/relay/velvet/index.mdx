## Character

The warm, authoritative, polished voice in the family. The Retrotron Nashville middle is a core voice — present and focused, not background texture — so it sits in the 5-way and earns its own selector positions. Think comping behind a singer, rich chord melody, and rhythm work where the guitar sets the center of gravity.

## Who it's for

Jazz, blues, soul, and R&B players who want a neck pickup with body and polish, and a middle with real presence — not just sparkle. If words like _round_, _authoritative_, and _controlled_ describe what you're after, Velvet is the model.
