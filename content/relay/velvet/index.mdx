## Voice and vibe

Velvet is the model you reach for when the room is small, the lights are low, and every note should land with warmth and confidence. The middle pickup is not a thin “in-between” — it is a Retrotron Nashville voice meant to sit in the mix like a good vocalist: full in the chest, clear in the phrase, never shrill. Neck and bridge humbuckers anchor the ends of the story; the center is where Velvet tells you who it is.

Picture comping behind a singer with half-closed eyes, or leading a trio where your chords need to speak without stepping on the bass. That is the emotional center this build is aimed at.

## Who falls in love with this

Players who live in jazz, blues, soul, and R&B — anyone who wants **presence without harshness**. If you describe your ideal tone with words like *round*, *authoritative*, *elegant*, and *controlled*, Velvet is the fantasy spec. It is for people who care as much about how the guitar feels in a band context as how it sounds alone in a bedroom.
