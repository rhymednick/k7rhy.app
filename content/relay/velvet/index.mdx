## Controls

**5-way blade**

1. Bridge
2. Bridge + middle
3. Middle
4. Neck + middle
5. Neck

**Tone (push-pull)** — Velvet focus contour.

## Character

Velvet is warm, fat, and deliberately unhurried. The Retrotron Nashville in the middle position isn't a transition pickup — it's a destination you can build a whole performance around. It belongs in the 5-way map as a named position, not a blend option, because it's that distinct and that usable. Think small room, late night, playing behind someone who's really singing.

## Who it's for

Jazz, blues, soul, and R&B players. If your target sound involves the word "round," this is the model. If you've ever wanted your middle position to be something you actually use instead of passing through, Velvet was designed around that.
