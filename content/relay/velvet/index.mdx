## Voice and character

Relay Velvet is the **warm, fat, classy club** model. This is not "Relay Lipstick, but jazzier." It has a different center of gravity: **authoritative, present, and polished** — the guitar that can lead the room without turning sharp or brittle. The middle pickup is a **Retrotron Nashville** voice meant to add **presence and focus**, not novelty, which is why Velvet belongs in the **5-way family**: the middle and its combinations are **core voices**, not a background layer.

Velvet should feel **poised**, not clever. Think comping behind a singer, rich chord melody, or rhythm work where the band orbits the guitarist in a small room.

## Who builds this

Players who want **a neck voice with body and polish**, a **middle voice with real presence** (not just sparkle), and **warm chord melody and elegant stage tone** — jazz, blues, soul, and R&B contexts where words like _round_, _authoritative_, and _controlled_ fit. If you describe your ideal as the band leaning on _you_ in a classy room, Velvet is the fantasy spec.
