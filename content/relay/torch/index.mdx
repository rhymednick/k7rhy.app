## Controls

**5-way blade**

1. Bridge
2. Bridge + middle
3. Middle
4. Neck + middle
5. Neck

**Tone (push-pull)** — Edge contour.

## Character

Torch is mid-forward, immediate, and a little rude — in the best way. The Mean 90 in the middle isn't there to add sparkle to the humbuckers; it's a distinct voice that earns its own real estate on the switch. You can lean on it for rhythm hooks, aggressive cleans, or anywhere you want the guitar to push back with some attitude. Less polished than Velvet, less airy than Arc — this one has an edge and isn't trying to hide it.

## Who it's for

Rock, pop, alternative, and modern country players. If you've ever wanted your guitar to feel like it's playing with you instead of just responding to you, this is the one.
