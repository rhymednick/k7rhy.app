## Controls

**5-way blade**

1. Bridge
2. Bridge + middle
3. Middle
4. Neck + middle
5. Neck

**Tone (push-pull)** — Edge contour.

## Character

Vocal mids, attitude, and immediacy. The Mean 90-class middle sits in the 5-way as a core position — a character you lean on for rhythm hooks, aggressive cleans, and modern rock energy, not polite background texture. Direct and emotionally forward; the guitar that talks back when you dig in.

## Who it's for

Rock, pop, alternative, and modern country players who want midrange that pushes back and a sound that feels immediate under the pick. The broadest non-metal appeal in the lineup.
