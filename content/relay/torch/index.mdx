## Voice and vibe

Torch is the model with **heat in the middle**. A P90-type center pickup brings a rude, immediate quality — the kind of voice that jumps forward when you dig in and relaxes when you ease off. It is not polite background texture; it is a character you can lean on for rhythm hooks, aggressive cleans, and modern rock energy without defaulting to scooped high-gain clichés.

Imagine riffs that need to speak in a dense mix, or rhythm parts that need attitude without turning the amp into a wall of fuzz.

## Who falls in love with this

Players drawn to **rock, pop, alternative, and modern country** — anyone who wants **midrange that sings** and a guitar that feels responsive under dynamic playing. If you like pickups that reward touch and make the amp feel like an extension of your picking hand, Torch is aimed at you.
