## Controls

**3-way toggle**

1. Bridge
2. Bridge + neck
3. Neck

**Volume (push-push)** — Adds the Hot Nashville middle layer.

**Tone (push-pull)** — Snap/bite contour.

## Character

Current is about attack and clarity. The Hot Nashville in the middle is there as an auxiliary voice — you add it via the volume push when you want to sharpen the edge and boost the upper mids, and it stays out of the way otherwise. Less bloom than Velvet, less air than Arc. The control layout reflects the design intent: three clean positions, one push to add the bite layer.

## Who it's for

Funk, pop, and rock rhythm players. If you spend most of your time in a groove context where sitting on top of the mix matters more than warmth or sustain, Current is designed for that job.
