## Controls

**3-way toggle**

1. Bridge
2. Bridge + neck
3. Neck

**Volume (push-push)** — Adds the Hot Nashville middle layer.

**Tone (push-pull)** — Snap/bite contour.

## Character

Punchy, cutting, and fast. The middle Hot Nashville is a curated auxiliary layer — engaged via the volume push — that adds snappy upper-mid presence and strong transient definition. Less warm than Velvet, less spacious than Arc. The sharpest non-metal tool in the lineup: percussive rhythm work with clarity and momentum.

## Who it's for

Funk, pop, and rock rhythm players who want speed, clarity, and edge — anyone who hears "tight" and "forward" as compliments and needs to sit on top of a busy groove without getting muddy.
