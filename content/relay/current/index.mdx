## Voice and vibe

Current is the **forward** one. It is tuned for attack and definition: notes that pop off the string and sit in a mix with upper-mid presence and a crisp transient. Think of it as the opposite of sleepy — built for players who need **clarity and momentum** when the band is busy and the pocket is tight.

Funk comping, percussive pop rhythm, and rock parts that need to slice without sounding harsh all point in this direction.

## Who falls in love with this

Players who prioritize **rhythm, articulation, and punch** — funk, pop, and rock rhythm guitarists who hear “tight” as a compliment. If Velvet is warmth and Torch is midrange fire, Current is the model you imagine when you need **immediate, cutting response** that still feels musical rather than clinical.

## Where this model stands today

Relay Current is **planned**, and the development timeline is less certain than for other entries in the lineup. The Relay platform body and shared build guides still describe how a Current would come together physically; electronics and sourcing will be documented when the design is ready.
