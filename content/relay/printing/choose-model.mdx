Every Relay guitar uses the **same printed body platform** — the same architecture, neck format, and three-pickup layout. What defines a **model** is not a different body file; it is the **pickup set and electronics identity** built around that shared foundation. When you choose a model, you are choosing how those three pickups are voiced and switched — the character pickup and the way it sits against the humbuckers (exact switching differs by model; see each model page).

For **effort, skills, and stage-by-stage difficulty** shared by every model, read [What building takes](/docs/relay/printing/build-expectations) next (or before you commit filament).

## Meet the lineup

Each model has a short landing page focused on **why you might want to build it** — tone, vibe, and musical context, not a parts list.

- [Relay Lipstick](/docs/relay/lipstick) — **Available.** Dual humbuckers on a 3-way foundation plus a lipstick for tonal shaping (blended in, not a stand-alone voice). The only model with a full instrument BOM, compatibility notes, and a released print bundle today.
- [Relay Velvet](/docs/relay/velvet) — Planned. Warm authority; jazz, soul, and club presence.
- [Relay Arc](/docs/relay/arc) — Planned. Chime, air, and spatial clarity for clean and ambient textures.
- [Relay Torch](/docs/relay/torch) — Planned. Vocal mids and contemporary energy; P90-type middle voice.
- [Relay Current](/docs/relay/current) — Planned. Punch, cut, and immediacy for rhythm-forward playing.
- [Relay Hammer](/docs/relay/hammer) — Planned. High-gain specialist; metal and hard rock.

Skim the pages that speak to you. There is nothing to buy — the point is to picture the instrument you want on the stand before you commit filament and weekends.

## What exists in the docs today

**Printing and body assembly** are documented at the **platform** level: same materials, same bonding workflow, same structural expectations for every model.

**Model-specific** documentation (full parts lists, compatibility, wiring tuned to that pickup set) tracks each release. Today, **Relay Lipstick** is the only model with that depth. Planned models link forward to shared platform guides for anything that already applies.

If you are building Lipstick, continue from here to [Printing overview](/docs/relay/printing/overview) for downloads and material notes, and use the Lipstick [Supply List](/docs/relay/lipstick/bom) and [What Will Fit](/docs/relay/lipstick/compatibility) before you order hardware.

If you are following a planned model, treat the platform printing and build sections as your reference for the body; watch that model’s page for updates as electronics and BOMs ship.

## Community input

The lineup on the [platform overview](/docs/relay) includes a voting tool for **planned** models — optional feedback that helps prioritize what gets built next. It does not change what is available today.
