## What this project is

A Relay is a **ground-up DIY electric guitar** built around a shared 3D-printed body platform. Model files and this documentation are free. Each model differs mainly in **pickups and electronics**; printing, body bonding, hardware, and setup follow the same structural path.

This is not a “swap this body onto any neck and hope” project. It is a full build with sourcing guidance, explained constraints, and a community behind it. The goal is a **working instrument** — one you understand well enough to finish, adjust, and if needed, hand to a professional at the right moment.

## Who this is for

You do not need to be an experienced luthier. For **any** Relay model, you do need to be comfortable with:

- 3D printing with structural materials (not PLA)
- Basic hand tools and patience during assembly
- Learning electronics fundamentals, or following a wiring diagram carefully
- Accepting that some steps may require outside help

If you have printed parts before but never turned a build into a playable guitar, this documentation is written with you in mind.

A successful build is an instrument capable of being set up and played — not necessarily one where you do every step yourself. My first Relay was assembled and set up by a luthier. That is a completely valid outcome.

## Before you commit

Pick a direction in the lineup from [Choosing a model](/relay/printing/choose-model). When you are ready to source and print, follow your model’s parts list and platform printing docs — for the only fully documented instrument today, that is [Relay Lipstick](/relay/lipstick) with its [Supply List](/relay/lipstick/bom) and [What Will Fit](/relay/lipstick/compatibility).

## If you get stuck

The <DiscordLink /> is the right place for build questions. Post photos, describe what you are seeing, and I will engage. This is not a formal support service, but it is an active community and I am in it.

## Remixers

If you arrived here from a derivative or remixed model, platform-level documentation applies to any build based on the Relay architecture. Constraints around neck dimensions, pickup pairing, and material choice are platform-level — they do not change with the remix. Please credit the original model in your derivative.
