<DecisionNote variant="caution" title="Use G/Flex 650 specifically — not generic epoxy">
G/Flex 650 is a flexible marine-grade epoxy that bonds well to plastics and remains slightly flexible when cured. Standard rigid epoxy can crack under the stress and vibration of a strung instrument. I've tested alternatives and come back to this every time.
</DecisionNote>
