## Pickups

These are [GFS Kwikplug-ready](https://www.guitarfetish.com) pickups. The Kwikplug connector system lets you swap pickups without resoldering — a real advantage on a first build. The specific models matter; see [Design Boundaries](/docs/relay/lipstick/electronics/design-boundaries) for why.

<BomSection>
    <BomItem title="Neck Humbucker — KPH115 (Classic II Alnico 2)" href="https://www.guitarfetish.com/KP--GFS-Classic-II-Alnico-2-Vintage-wound-Humbuckers-Black-Kwikplug%E2%84%A2-Ready_p_21913.html" source="Guitar Fetish" itemKey="gfs-neck-pickup" fallback="$37.00" specific>
        Vintage-wound Alnico 2 — warm and clear in the neck position. Pairs well with the lipstick without fighting it.
    </BomItem>
    <BomItem title="Middle Lipstick — KPS41 (Pro Tube)" href="https://www.guitarfetish.com/Pro-Tube-Lipstick-Pickups-Gloss-black--Kwikplug%E2%84%A2-Ready_p_21995.html" source="Guitar Fetish" itemKey="gfs-lipstick-pickup" fallback="$32.00" specific>
        The centerpiece of this design. The circuit is balanced around this specific pickup's output level and impedance characteristics. Substituting a different lipstick may work but expect a different tonal balance.
    </BomItem>
    <BomItem title="Bridge Humbucker — KPH65 (Vintage 59 Alnico V)" href="https://www.guitarfetish.com/Vintage-59-Classic-Alnico-V-Humbucker-Black_p_21820.html" source="Guitar Fetish" itemKey="gfs-bridge-pickup" fallback="$37.00" specific>
        Slightly hotter than the neck but still in the moderate-output range the circuit requires. High-output pickups overwhelm the lipstick — do not substitute above ~9kΩ DC resistance.
    </BomItem>
</BomSection>

## Electronics

<BomSection>
    <BomItem title="Push-Push Pots (×2)" href="https://amzn.to/4bsb448" source="Amazon" itemKey="push-push-pots" fallback="$15.00"
        substitution="Standard 500k pots work for volume and tone if you don't want coil splitting. You'll lose the split function but the rest of the circuit is unaffected. If you want splits, these specific pots are the right choice."
    >
        These serve as both volume controls and coil splits — push to toggle. Keeps the control layout clean without adding extra switches.
    </BomItem>
    <BomItem title="Selector Switch" href="https://amzn.to/40LRuJY" source="Amazon" itemKey="selector-switch" fallback="$8.00" specific>
        The 5-way switch provides specific series and parallel combinations that make the lipstick usable in each position. Do not substitute a 3-way.
    </BomItem>
    <BomItem title="Tone Capacitor" href="https://amzn.to/3NEJ18n" source="Amazon" itemKey="tone-capacitor" fallback="$10.00"
        substitution="Cap value is a tonal preference. The listed value gives a warm but not muddy roll-off. Smaller value = cuts higher frequencies; larger = cuts lower. Caps are cheap — experiment freely."
    />
    <BomItem title="Treble Bleed" href="https://amzn.to/4bUwS8x" source="Amazon" itemKey="treble-bleed" fallback="$15.00" specific>
