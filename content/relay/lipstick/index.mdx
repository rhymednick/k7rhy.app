## Voice and vibe

Relay Lipstick is the **signature model** in the family: the one with the clearest “Relay” identity. **Neck and bridge humbuckers** are the familiar backbone — riffs, chords, and weight. The **middle lipstick** is not a stand-alone destination you park on. It is a **musically curated alternate layer** you bring in when you want a second character: more air, detail, or a shifted spectral balance on top of the buckers — polish and personality without turning the guitar into a gimmick.

Behavior should feel like a **normal player’s guitar first**. The lipstick path should read as a **second identity of the same instrument**, not a special effect.

## Who falls in love with this

Players who want **strong clean articulation**, **expressive pick response**, a bridge that can still get a little angry, and **touch sensitivity and personality** more than sheer output or maximum tonal coverage. If you live in **blues, rock, alternative, and indie** and like tweaking tone with your hands and ears, Lipstick is aimed at you.
