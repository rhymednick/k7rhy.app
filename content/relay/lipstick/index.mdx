## Controls

**3-way toggle**

1. Bridge
2. Bridge + neck
3. Neck

**Volume (push-push)** — Engages the lipstick layer with bridge partial split and level compensation.

**Tone (push-pull)** — Alternate voicing contour.

## Character

Neck and bridge humbuckers are the backbone — riffs, chords, and weight. The middle lipstick is a curated alternate layer: more air, more detail, a shifted spectral balance that reads as a second identity of the same instrument when engaged, not as a special effect.

## Who it's for

Blues, rock, alternative, and indie players who want articulation, touch sensitivity, and expressive pick response over maximum output or wall-to-wall tonal coverage.
