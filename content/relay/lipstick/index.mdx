## Controls

**3-way toggle**

1. Bridge
2. Bridge + neck
3. Neck

**Volume (push-push)** — Adds the lipstick layer. The bridge also gets a partial split and level compensation so everything integrates cleanly.

**Tone (push-pull)** — Alternate voicing contour.

## Character

Neck and bridge humbuckers handle the familiar work — riffs, chords, enough weight to push an amp. The middle lipstick gives you a different instrument on the same body: brighter, airier, with a spectral balance that complements the humbuckers instead of competing with them. You're not toggling to a novelty; you're toggling to an alternate version of the same guitar.

## Who it's for

Blues, rock, alternative, and indie players who want a guitar that responds to how they play — lighter touches getting lighter sounds, harder picks getting more. Output isn't the priority here; feel is.
