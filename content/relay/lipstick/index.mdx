## Voice and vibe

Relay Lipstick treats the **two humbuckers** as the backbone: neck, bridge, and both — the familiar ground where riffs and chords live. The **lipstick pickup** is not a third “destination” you park on alone. It is a **shaping layer** you bring in when you want more air, detail, or a shifted spectral balance on top of whatever the humbuckers are already doing — polish and character, not a separate instrument hiding in the middle route.

Think of it as having a great dual-humbucker guitar first, with an extra voice you can **steer into** the sound when the song asks for it — brighter, more present, or more complex — without giving up the weight and authority of the buckers underneath.

## Who falls in love with this

Players who live in **blues, rock, alternative, and indie** and already like **humbuckers for body and punch**, but wish they could **open up** the top end or add a distinctive color without reaching for another guitar. If you like tweaking tone with your hands and ears rather than collecting radically different pickup voices, Lipstick is aimed at you.
