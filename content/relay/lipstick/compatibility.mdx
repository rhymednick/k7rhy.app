The Relay Lipstick body is designed around specific hardware dimensions. Most of these aren't negotiable — the body was modeled around them. This page tells you what those dimensions are and what to watch for when sourcing parts.

## Neck

## Pickups

Pickup routing dimensions and ring mount spacing are designed around the <Term id="gfs">GFS</Term> Kwikplug housings listed in the BOM. Other pickups in standard humbucker and single-coil lipstick form factors should fit the cavities, but the Kwikplug connector system is why I use <Term id="gfs">GFS</Term> specifically — it makes future swaps much easier.

See [Design Boundaries](/relay/electronics/design-boundaries) for the electrical constraints on pickup substitution.

## Controls

The control cavity is sized for the push-push pots and selector switch listed in the BOM. Standard full-size (CTS-style) pots also fit. Mini pots are not recommended — the cavity walls are not thick enough to support them securely.

## What can be different

- **Tuner style:** Any standard 6-in-line tuner with matching headstock hole diameter (typically 10mm) works. Locking tuners are recommended but not required.
- **Strap buttons:** Any standard strap button.
- **Knobs:** Any knob that fits the pot shaft (split shaft or solid shaft depending on pots chosen).
