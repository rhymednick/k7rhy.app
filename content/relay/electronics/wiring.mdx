This page walks through the complete wiring process. Read [Wiring & Electronics Overview](/docs/relay/electronics/overview) first so you understand what you're building before you start soldering.

If you've never soldered before, this is a learnable skill but takes practice. Spend time on practice joints on scrap wire before working on the actual components.

## Tools and supplies

- Soldering iron (25–40W, fine tip)
- Rosin-core solder (60/40 or 63/37)
- Flux pen (recommended)
- Helping hands or PCB vise
- Wire strippers
- Heat shrink tubing (assorted)
- Multimeter (essential for testing)

## Testing before installation

Before installing anything in the body, test the circuit on the bench:

1. Use a multimeter to verify continuity from each pickup through to the output jack for each **3-way** humbucker position and with the **lipstick blend** engaged as wired
2. Check that no switch lugs or positions are shorted to ground unintentionally
3. Plug in to an amp and tap each pickup with a screwdriver to verify signal
