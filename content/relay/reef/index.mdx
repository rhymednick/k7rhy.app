## Controls

**5-way blade**

1. Bridge humbucker
2. Bridge + middle lipstick
3. Middle lipstick
4. Middle + neck lipstick — signature two-lipstick sound
5. Neck lipstick

**Concentric tone**

- Top ring — bridge humbucker tone
- Bottom ring — lipstick subsystem tone (middle and/or neck, depending on position)

## Character

Reef is built around a tension that usually requires two guitars: a humbucker with real weight in the bridge slot, and a pair of lipstick pickups that do the glassy, piano-like thing Danelectro players have been chasing since the 50s. They don't blend into each other — they're genuinely different voice families. The 5-way maps them predictably, with position 4 as the moment where both lipsticks run together. The stacked tone pot keeps them independently shapeable so you're not compromising one to get the other.

## Who it's for

Indie, surf, alt-country, and shoegaze players who love the Danelectro sound but have been frustrated by Danelectros. Also good for studio players who want two genuinely distinct voices in one guitar for the same session.
