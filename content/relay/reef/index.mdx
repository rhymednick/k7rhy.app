## Controls

**5-way blade**

1. Bridge humbucker
2. Bridge + middle lipstick
3. Middle lipstick
4. Middle + neck lipstick — signature two-lipstick sound
5. Neck lipstick

**Concentric tone**

- Top ring — bridge humbucker tone
- Bottom ring — lipstick subsystem tone (middle and/or neck, depending on position)

## Character

Two voice families in one body. The bridge humbucker provides warmth, sustain, and enough output to push an amp into bloom. The middle and neck lipsticks deliver glassy, piano-like Danelectro articulation — transparent and pedal-forward. A standard 5-way maps them across five positions, with position 4 as the signature two-lipstick slot.

## Who it's for

Indie, surf, alt-country, and shoegaze players who want Danelectro-style glass alongside a bridge that can drive an amp. Well-suited for pedal-forward rigs and studio work where tonal contrast between two genuinely different voices matters.
