## Voice and character

Reef is built around contrast. The **bridge humbucker** is the workhorse: warmth, sustain, and enough output to push an amp into bloom. The **lipstick pair** — middle and neck — delivers the glassy, piano-like articulation people associate with Danelectro-style instruments. They are two genuinely different **voice families** sharing one body, not two similar pickups balancing each other.

The control story follows an **HSS-style skeleton** on a **standard 5-way blade**: honest bridge and neck ends, a familiar bridge-adjacent blend, a **center slot** you can tune for personality over time, **position 4 as the star** for the two-lipstick sound, and a true neck lipstick in position 5. That keeps the ends easy to read while the middle carries any future “interesting” routing without breaking Strat-like expectations at 1 or 5.

**Stacked tone:** one control shapes the **bridge humbucker**; the other shapes the **lipstick subsystem** (however those pickups are combined in a given selector position). You can darken the humbucker without dulling the lipsticks, or roll the lipstick side without robbing the bridge of bite.

## Who builds this

Makers who want Danelectro-style glass and articulation but also need a bridge pickup with real weight. Players drawn to indie textures, surf reverb, alt-country, and shoegaze — and anyone who wants **pedal-forward** transparency on the lipstick side. If you like a **familiar 5-way map** with a clear “go here for both lipsticks” moment, Reef is aimed at you.

## Controls

**5-way blade (HSS-style map)**

1. Bridge humbucker alone  
2. Bridge humbucker + middle lipstick  
3. Middle lipstick alone _(prototype default — mirrors familiar HSS “middle single” logic, low cognitive load)_  
4. Middle lipstick + neck lipstick — **signature two-lipstick sound**  
5. Neck lipstick alone  

Reef is not on the simple **master volume + master tone** grid the other models share: the concentric tone keeps the humbucker and lipstick network independently shapeable. Wiring and parts will be documented with the Reef-specific guide when the model is validated.

**Concentric tone**

- **Bridge humbucker** tone — high-end and warmth for positions that include the humbucker  
- **Lipstick subsystem** tone — shaping for any position built from the middle and/or neck lipsticks
