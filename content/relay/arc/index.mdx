## Voice and vibe

Arc is the wide-open sky in the lineup. It is built around shimmer and dimension: notes that hang in the air and decay with character instead of collapsing into mud. The middle pickup is a Dream 180 voice — a deliberate choice for players who want **clarity without sterility**. Neck humbucker and a clear-voiced bridge frame a palette that feels tall and bright without tipping into ice-pick territory.

Think clean arpeggios that still have body, ambient layers that need separation, or country-influenced lines that need sparkle and definition.

## Who falls in love with this

Players who live in **clean pop, indie, ambient, and country** — anyone who chases *space*, *ring*, and *articulation* first. If you like pedals that create washes and delays that fill a room, Arc is the guitar you imagine under that soundscape. It is for people who hear “warm” and “open” as compatible, not opposite.
