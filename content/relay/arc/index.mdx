## Controls

**5-way blade**

1. Bridge
2. Bridge + middle
3. Middle
4. Neck + middle
5. Neck

**Tone (push-pull)** — Arc air contour.

## Character

Width, shimmer, and clarity without thinning out. The Dream 180 middle sits in the 5-way as a core voice — chords stay separated and dimensional, single lines feel lit rather than ice-picky. Built for spatial clarity: arpeggios, ambient layers, and country-influenced lines that need air and body together.

## Who it's for

Clean pop, indie, ambient, and country players who want openness and separation without sacrificing weight — especially anyone running delays or reverbs who needs the guitar to stay articulate underneath the effect.
