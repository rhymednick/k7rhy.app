**Tone (push-pull)** — Arc air contour.

## Character

Arc is the open, spatial model — chords that breathe, single notes that ring out without biting. The Dream 180 in the middle isn't just a blend option; it's a genuine tonal destination with enough character to carry a whole section. The goal was clarity without brittleness: the kind of guitar that sounds like it has a natural reverb tail even when the amp is dry.

## Who it's for

Clean pop, indie, ambient, and country players. Particularly good for anyone who runs a lot of time-based effects — Arc doesn't disappear into reverb and delay the way darker guitars do. It stays present and articulate underneath.
